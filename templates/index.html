Why use Deteggt?

Parasites such as Roundworms are a common issues in the agriculture industry, often causing illness and lowering production among farm animals. While dewormers are a common practice to eliminate parasites, excessive usage leads to parasites developing resistance.

Deteggt utilizes the YOLOv8 model to analyze an inputted image of an animalâ€™s fecal sample to determine the number of parasitical eggs present. Using the information presented, farmers may make informed decisions on whether or not to use dewormers on their animals.

Deteggt should not be used as a final deciding factor. It merely exists to assist you in making informed decisions.
